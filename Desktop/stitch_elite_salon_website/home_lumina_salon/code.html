Founded on the principles of holistic renewal, Lumina is more than just a salon. It is a curated escape from the urban tempo—a space where time slows down and focus returns to the self.

Every service at Lumina is a bespoke ritual. We combine world-class technique with organic botanical elements to ensure that you leave our sanctuary feeling revitalized, inside and out. Our master artisans are dedicated to the craft of subtle, sophisticated transformation.
